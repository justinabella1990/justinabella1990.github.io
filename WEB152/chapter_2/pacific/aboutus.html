Meet the Smiths

We are the Smith family and we are proud to share our love for nature and hospitality with you. Our family has been welcoming guests to our luxury nature resort since 1925, offering a unique and unforgettable vacation experience.
When you stay with us, you'll be staying in one of our cozy and comfortable yurts, nestled in the heart of the stunning Pacific Northwest. Our yurts are designed to blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings, providing a true immersion in nature while still enjoying the comforts of modern amenities.
One of the highlights of staying with us is the abundance of outdoor activities available right at your doorstep. Whether you're an avid kayaker, a bird-watching enthusiast, or a hiker looking for stunning vistas and hidden trails, Pacific Trails has something for everyone. Our knowledgeable staff are always on hand to help you plan the perfect itinerary, and we offer guided tours and experiences to help you get the most out of your stay.
At Pacific Trails, we take pride in being an old family business, passed down from generation to generation. We believe that our deep connection to the land and our commitment to sustainable practices are what make us stand out in the luxury resort industry. We take care to minimize our impact on the environment while still providing top-notch service and comfort for our guests.

We invite you to join us at Pacific Trails and experience the beauty and wonder of the Pacific Northwest for yourself. Check out our gallery of images to see the stunning vistas, cozy yurts, and exciting activities that await you!
